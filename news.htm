Research

Our group's research is centered around multimessenger astrophysics
which combines multiple cosmic messengers (e.g. gravitational waves
gamma ray bursts, neutrinos) to understand more from astrophysical
phenomena. We have done work in collaboration with LIGO and IceCube.
We study colliding black holes, neutron star mergers, and high energy
